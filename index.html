Quick Wash Pro Support

Welcome to the Quick Wash Pro support page! Quick Wash Pro is a marketplace app connecting washers and clients for easy laundry and dry cleaning service booking.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Quick Wash Pro?
Quick Wash Pro is a mobile app that connects clients with laundry and dry cleaning services in their area. It allows clients to easily find, book, schedule pickups/drop-offs, and pay for cleaning services. Washers (laundry service providers) can use the app to manage their orders, showcase their services, and grow their client base.
How do I book a service?

To book a service, simply browse washers in your area, view their profiles and services offered, and select the services and pickup/drop-off times that work for you. You can then securely book and pay through the app.

How do I contact a washer?
You can contact a washer directly through the app's messaging feature. You can discuss your specific needs, instructions, and preferences before booking an appointment.
I'm a washer. How do I sign up?
Washers can create a profile directly within the Quick Wash Pro app. Provide your information, showcase your services, set your pricing and availability, and specify your pickup/drop-off areas to start accepting bookings.
What if I have a problem with my order or service?
Please contact our support team using the email address below. We will work to resolve any issues you may encounter. We also encourage you to contact the washer directly to discuss your concerns.

Troubleshooting

The app is crashing.
First, try closing and restarting the app. If that doesn't work, try restarting your device. If the problem persists, please contact support with details about your device and what you were doing when the app crashed.
I can't log in.
Make sure you're using the correct email address and password. If you've forgotten your password, use the "Forgot Password" link on the login screen. If you're still having trouble, contact support.
I'm not seeing washers in my area.
Ensure that location services are enabled for the Quick Wash Pro app in your device settings. Also, some areas may have limited availability of washers. We are constantly expanding our network.
I'm a washer and not receiving booking requests.
Double-check that your profile is complete and that your availability and service areas are correctly configured. Also, ensure your notifications are enabled in the app settings.
